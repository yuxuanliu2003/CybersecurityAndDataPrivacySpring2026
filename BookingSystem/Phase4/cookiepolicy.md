# Cookie Policy

## What Are Cookies
Cookies are small text files stored on a user’s device to maintain session information.

## Cookies Used by This System
This booking system uses cookies for:
- Session management
- User authentication
- Security monitoring

## Purpose of Cookies
Cookies help maintain login sessions and ensure secure communication between the user and the server.

## Managing Cookies
Users can delete or block cookies through their browser settings.

## Contact
Questions about cookies can be directed to the system administrator.
